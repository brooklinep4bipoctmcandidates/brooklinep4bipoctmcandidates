Can I Vote Absentee?

 

  • Absent from Brookline on Election Day
  • Physical Disability
  • Religious Beliefs
  • Concern about COVID-19

 

How to Vote Absentee?

 

There are two ways to vote absentee:
  1. Mail to Town Clerk before April 27, 2022.
  2. Drop off at Town Clerk before noon on May 2nd

 

Need Help?

When and Where to Vote

 

  • There is no Early In-Person voting this year.
  • Unless you are voting absentee, all voting this spring is in-person.

 

Go Absentee

 

  • Go by Town Hall
  • Fill out an Absentee Ballot Request
  • They will hand you a ballot and you can fill it out right there
