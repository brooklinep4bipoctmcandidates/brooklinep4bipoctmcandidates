Who Are We?

We are people of color who are running for election to Brookline Town Meeting in one of two new Majority Minority voting precincts
- Precinct 4!!

What is Plus?

Plus + stands for low-income. Some BIPOC folks are low-income. Others are not, but there are a lot of priorities that BIPOC and low-income folks share.

We Believe

 

In electing a diverse group of Town Meeting members to represent the views and voices of those who have not historically participated in Town government.
